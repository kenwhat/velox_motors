Engineering Philosophy

Why Velox
Drives Different

01

Raw Performance

Every Velox vehicle is tuned on real race circuits. Our engineers obsess over lap times, not just spec sheets. From turbo response to suspension geometry — we engineer for the visceral thrill of driving.

02

Precision Craftsmanship

Handstitched leather, milled aluminum, and carbon fiber panels are assembled by artisans in our facility. Every interior is built with tolerance standards tighter than a Swiss watch.

03

Intelligent Safety

Our adaptive driver assistance suite uses dual-camera vision and LiDAR fusion to anticipate hazards before you react. Safety isn't optional at Velox — it's the foundation everything is built on.

04

Future-Ready EV

Our proprietary Helios battery platform delivers 500+ km range with 20-minute rapid charging. Software-defined architecture means your Velox improves over time — it gets smarter as you drive.

Our Story

Forged
in Racing,
Refined
for the Road.

Velox was born in 2010 from a single garage in Turin where three engineers bet everything on one belief: that a car built for a race track could also be the most refined thing you'd ever own. Fifteen years, four championship titles, and one hundred thousand delivered cars later, that bet paid off.

Today we build vehicles for those who refuse to compromise — drivers who want performance without apology and elegance without excess.
